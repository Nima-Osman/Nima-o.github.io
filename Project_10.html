Airbnb Business Analysis: Summary

Background

Since its inception in 2008, Airbnb has transformed travel by offering unique and personalized experiences. The Airbnb NYC dataset (AB_NYC_2019.csv) provides detailed information about listings in New York City for 2019, including host details, geographical availability, and metrics for analysis and prediction.

Objective

The objective of this assignment is to propose and answer a business-relevant question using data science techniques. This includes data pre-processing, exploratory data analysis (EDA), statistical analysis, and machine learning to deliver actionable insights.

Tasks

Task 1: Business Analytic Question

Propose an analytic question relevant to Airbnb. For example: "Is there a noticeable difference in bookings across neighborhoods, and what factors influence these differences?"

Task 2: Data Analysis

  • Perform EDA to uncover patterns and trends.
  • Preprocess data to address inconsistencies and missing values.
  • Apply statistical and machine learning techniques, such as clustering, to answer the posed question.

Task 3: Analytical Report

Prepare a concise 1000-word report with clear visualizations and descriptions, aimed at Airbnb's executive board. The report should stand alone, with Python code included as an appendix.

Assessment Criteria

  • Substantive Question: 5% - Relevance and depth of the proposed question.
  • Rationale: 5% - Arguments and reasoning for the question's importance.
  • Business Impact: 5% - Expected value and insights for Airbnb.
  • Methodology: 40% - EDA, preprocessing, and machine learning implementation.
  • Visualizations: 40% - Quality and clarity of graphs and charts.
  • Presentation: 5% - Overall structure and readability.

Learning Outcomes

  • Understand the challenges and applications of machine learning with diverse datasets.
  • Develop skills to work effectively in a team, adopting real-world roles and perspectives.

Conclusion

This assignment combines data science techniques with business acumen to provide actionable insights for Airbnb. By addressing meaningful questions, leveraging data analytics, and presenting results clearly, students gain valuable experience in bridging technical skills with real-world applications.
